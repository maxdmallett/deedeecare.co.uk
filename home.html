About

At Dee Dee Care, we understand the changes which can affect the physical and mental well-being of a family. We value the importance of feeling comfortable in your home when inviting support.

Our team has combined years of experience in different care settings, such as Dementia & Alzheimer's, Learning Disabilities, Mental Health, Hoarding, One-to-one support and Stroke & Diabetes. The team are all fully DBS checked and also have liability insurance.
